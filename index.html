Areas

Research Focus

🧠

Multimodal Learning & LLMs

Designing MEDI-SLATE and MILU — benchmarks for medical imaging lecture understanding. Built ALIVE, a content-aware avatar-lecture interaction engine. Introduced SlideChain for blockchain-anchored semantic provenance and N-ReLU activation.

🤖

AI Safety & Synthetic Agent Systems

Designed OpenClaw, an agent-only social network (Moltbook) to study risky instruction propagation. Developed ADAPT, an AI-driven decentralized publishing framework with a U.S. provisional patent. Investigating emergent self-regulation in synthetic societies.

🔗

Secure Information & Blockchain

Blockchain-based frameworks for multi-party skyline queries, academic credential verification, medical data sharing, and GDPR-compliant personal data management using Hyperledger Fabric and IPFS.

📊

Machine Learning & NLP

Sentiment analysis and emotion recognition for Bangla text. Adversarial robustness for text classifiers. CNN-based COVID-19 detection and genomic feature-based pandemic prediction.
